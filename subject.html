🕵️ Who Threw a Cake at the Monalisa? 🎂

목격자 1 / 4

🎨 Fun Fact

'Monalisa'는 콜롬비아 화가 Fernando Botero의 그림으로, Leonardo da Vinci의 'Mona Lisa'를 패러디한 작품이에요.

Last Saturday, someone threw a cake at the Monalisa in the Botero Museum in Bogota, Colombia. There were four eyewitnesses. What did they say? Read the following, and find the criminal.

📚 Key Vocabulary

threw (던졌다) eyewitness (목격자) criminal (범인) following (다음의) museum (박물관)
Q0 Where did the incident happen? (사건이 어디서 일어났나요?)
👩 Ann Jones
a visitor (관람객)

"I was looking at the Monalisa, and someone threw a cake at the painting. I turned around and saw an old man. He was standing in front of a wheelchair. I'm about 170 cm tall, and he was a little taller than me."

📚 Key Vocabulary

painting (그림) turned around (돌아봤다) wheelchair (휠체어) taller (더 키가 큰) in front of (~앞에)
Q1 What was Ann doing when the incident happened? (사건이 일어났을 때 Ann은 무엇을 하고 있었나요?)
Q2 How tall was the old man compared to Ann? (그 노인은 Ann과 비교해서 키가 어땠나요?)
Q3 Where was the old man standing? (그 노인은 어디에 서 있었나요?)
🧹 Carlos Diaz
a janitor (청소부)

"An old man with gray hair was running away, and something fell off his head. It was his wig. I ran after him, but I couldn't catch him. He was faster than me. In fact, the old man was not old. He was a young man with long brown hair."

📚 Key Vocabulary

running away (도망가는) fell off (떨어졌다) wig (가발) catch (잡다) in fact (사실은)
Q4 What fell off the old man's head? (그 노인의 머리에서 무엇이 떨어졌나요?)
Q5 Why couldn't Carlos catch the man? (Carlos는 왜 그 남자를 잡을 수 없었나요?)
Q6 What color was the young man's real hair? (그 젊은 남자의 진짜 머리색은 무엇이었나요?)
👮 Diego Perez
a guard (경비원)

"I went to the crime scene, and there were pieces of cake all over the painting. There was also a wheelchair near the painting, and I found a cake box next to the wheelchair. The box was from Camila's Bakery."

📚 Key Vocabulary

crime scene (범죄 현장) pieces (조각들) all over (~전체에) next to (~옆에) bakery (빵집)
Q7 What did Diego find all over the painting? (Diego는 그림 전체에서 무엇을 발견했나요?)
Q8 What did Diego find next to the wheelchair? (Diego는 휠체어 옆에서 무엇을 발견했나요?)
Q9 Where was the cake box from? (케이크 상자는 어디서 온 것이었나요?)
🧁 Camila Santos
the owner of Camila's Bakery (베이커리 사장)

"Last Friday, a young man came in. I spoke to him in Spanish, but he didn't understand me. He spoke only English. We had a lot of different cakes, but he just wanted the smallest one. We sold only one cake that day, so I remember him clearly. Oh, he had blue eyes."

📚 Key Vocabulary

came in (들어왔다) understand (이해하다) different (다른) smallest (가장 작은) clearly (명확히)
Q10 What language did the young man speak? (그 젊은 남자는 어떤 언어를 말했나요?)
Q11 Why does Camila remember the man clearly? (Camila는 왜 그 남자를 명확히 기억하나요?)
Q12 What color were the man's eyes? (그 남자의 눈은 무슨 색이었나요?)
